PHYSICS-[ELECTROSTATIC CHARGES & FIELDS]

*[NOTES]*

* ELECTRIC CHARGES

~The property of protons and electrons, which gives rise to electric force b/w them is called electric charge.

* COLOUMBS LAW

~The force of interaction b/w two stationary point charges is directly proportional to product of charges and inversely to square of the distance b/w them

* ELECTRIC DIPOLE

~The systyem of two charges having same magnitudes but opposite signs iss known as electric dipole.
